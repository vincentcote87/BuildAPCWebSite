Why Build Your Own PC

Did you know?Building your own computer from parts is not just for nerds and PC enthusiasts anymore. With a little guidance and knowhow anyone can build a PC, your grandma, the captain of the football team, your neighbour, and even you! Yes YOU can build a PC!!

Make it yours! Building a custom PC gives you the ultimate freedom to make it exactly what you want it to be, from small form factor home theater PC to a behemoth custom water cooling gaming PC, you name it!

Save some dough! Your money goes much further when building your own PC, you can get a cheaper computer or higher quality parts for the price of an off the shelf model. Building your own PC is often cheaper then buying an off the shelf PC, lets break it down!

Look what you built! Carefully choosing all your parts and assembling together yourself gives you a sort of pride, you made this! It also means you have a much better understanding of what each part do which will help you in the future when your PC starts to get dated and you want to upgrade parts of it.

Upgrading Parts! With an off the shelf pre-built computer, most people never bother even opening it up to see whatâ€™s inside. This can often lead to people replacing the entire computer for an entire new system once it starts to show its age, this is wasteful, expensive, and unnecessary. As your custom build PC starts to show its age you are more likely to simply swap out the individual parts that need the upgrades overtime. You can have your very own continuously evolving and growing PC, they grow up so fast!

In the end you will save money, learn a new skill (maybe a new hobby?), and have a kick ass custom PC that will make your friends jealous.
